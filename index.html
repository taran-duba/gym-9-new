Gym 9 provides a family environment where we are dedicated to keep you fit and healthy through a range of integrated services that includes Zumba, martial arts, diet & nutrition, weight training, personal training, strength and conditioning provided by exceptionally skilled trainers. We make sure that we imbibe the idea of celebrating health and fitness in every individual coming to our family. We completed are compliant about the precautions that are needed to be taken seeing the current situation for Covid-19. Our all three branches are sanitized, temperature check and oxygen saturation are performed on daily bases. For security reasons a proper biomenteric is performed. Be part of the elite!
